# Rental Market Analyzer

Tell me what you're looking for in an apartment - city, budget, commute destinations, lifestyle needs, whatever comes to mind. I'll ask a few follow-up questions, then search and research real listings on your behalf.

The more specific you are about trade-offs (e.g. "I'd pay more for a shorter commute" or "I have a dog"), the better I can filter and rank results for you.
